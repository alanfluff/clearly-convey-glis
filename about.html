Me

I became somewhat obsessed with semantic HTML, and with CSS, starting in 2001 and that obsession only got worse when I read Designing with Web Standards by Jeffrey Zeldman in 2003. Hungry for more I moved on to Web Standards Solutions by Dan Cederholm in 2004, and CSS: The Definitive Guide by Eric Meyer.

I've now channeled these early web obsessions to focus on building websites, and auditing websites for accessibility.
